Biography

Dr. Jingshu Chen is assistant professor of Computer Science at Oakland University. Before she joined Oakland University at 2017, she worked at FDA as ORISE researcher and INRIA as postdoc researchers. She earned her Ph.D degree in Computer Science with Dr. Sandeep Kulkanir from Michigan State University at 2013. She obtained her master degree and bachelar degree from Chinese Academy of Sciences and Xiamen University, respectively.

Dr. Chen's research interests include:

  • Software Defined System

  • Formal modeling, analysis and repair for Software Reliability

  • Learning based System Analysis and Prediction
