Jingshu Chen

Research

We are increasingly reliant on software to monitor and control almost every corner of the human world, from large-scale infrastructures, a variety of vehicles, to small implants inside human bodies. To underpin this trend, Jingshu's research focuses on the reliability, security, and performance of software in emerging application domains, including (1) cyber-physical systems (CPS), (2) Internet-of-Things (IoTs), (3) cloud systems, and (4) autonomous systems. Analyzing and ensuring software properties in these domains is often highly challenging due to the scale, complexity, and resource constraints of the system where software runs. To tackle these challenges, her research philosophy is an interdisciplinary one across software analysis, machine learning, signal processing, and systems.
